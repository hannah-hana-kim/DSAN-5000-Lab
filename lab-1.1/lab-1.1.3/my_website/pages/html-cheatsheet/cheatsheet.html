How to make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Easy Recipe

You’re going to start by melting the butter. The butter shouldn’t be sizzling hot. If it is about 90% melted, and then you’ll whisk it until it finishes melting.
Once the butter has melted, whisk in the sugar until well-combined. Mix in the vanilla extract and egg.
Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in just until combined. Stir in the chocolate chips, and then scoop the dough.
Place about 2 inches apart on the prepared baking sheet. Bake until the cookies are set on the edges but slightly underdone in the middle, about 7-10 minutes.
Slightly under-baking the cookies helps to keep them soft. Since oven temperatures can vary, you may find that you need to adjust the baking times.
